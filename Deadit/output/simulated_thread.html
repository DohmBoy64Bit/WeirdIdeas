How do you keep your horde focused during "Fourth of July"?

Every year it's the same thing. Survivors set off fireworks, and half my horde just stands there staring at the sky with their jaws hanging open (literally, for some). We missed a prime buffet at the park because everyone was too busy watching the sky explosions.

I've tried roaring, I've tried pushing them, I even threw a fresh arm in the opposite direction. Nothing works! Does anyone have a training method to get them to ignore loud noises and shiny lights? It's embarrassing to lose a horde to a sparkler.
